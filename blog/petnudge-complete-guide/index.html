PetNudge is more than just a pet ID tag app. It's a complete companion for modern pet owners who want to keep their furry friends safe and healthy. Let's explore everything PetNudge can do for you and your pet.

🚨 Lost Mode

When your pet goes missing, activate Lost Mode with one tap. This is what makes PetNudge different from regular ID tags.

What finders see in Lost Mode:

An urgent "LOST PET" alert, your contact info with one-tap calling, and any reward message you've set. They know immediately this pet needs to get home.

  • Instant one-tap calling to reach you immediately
  • Scan tracking shows when and where your tag was scanned
  • Optional reward message to motivate finders
  • Activate or deactivate remotely from anywhere

💉 Health Records

Keep all your pet's health information in one secure place. Never forget a vaccination date or medication dose again.

  • Track vaccinations with expiration dates
  • Log medications with dosage schedules
  • Monitor weight over time with charts
  • Record vet visits and notes
  • Store your microchip number

🔔 Smart Reminders

Life gets busy. PetNudge makes sure you never miss important dates for your pet's care.

  • Vaccination reminders before they expire
  • Medication alerts at the right time
  • Vet appointment notifications (1 week, 3 days, 1 day, 1 hour before)

📄 PDF Health Reports

Need to show your pet's health history to a vet or for travel? Generate professional PDF reports with one tap.

Perfect for:

Vet visits, international travel, boarding facilities, pet insurance claims, or moving to a new home.

🔒 Privacy First

Your pet's health data is personal. PetNudge is built with privacy as a core principle.

  • All health data stays on YOUR device — never uploaded to servers
  • Face ID / Touch ID protection available
  • Finders only see contact info — never health records
  • No tracking, no ads, no data selling

🐾 Multiple Pets

Have more than one pet? Manage all of them in one app. Each pet gets their own profile, health records, and NFC tag.
