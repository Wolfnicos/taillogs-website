The Problem

Traditional pet ID has failed

Microchips and engraved tags aren't enough when your pet goes missing.

🔬

Microchips require a vet visit

If someone finds your pet, they need to take it to a vet or shelter with a scanner. Most people don't know this or won't do it.

🏷️

Engraved tags get outdated

Phone numbers change. Tags get worn and unreadable. You can't update them remotely.

The Solution

Modern pet ID that actually works

Instant contact through technology everyone already has.

📡

NFC Tag

Tap to call. Works on all modern phones instantly. No app needed for finders.

🏷️

Custom Medal

3D printed in France with your pet's name. Waterproof and durable.

🚨

Lost Mode

Activate remotely. Finders see urgent alert with reward info.

How It Works

3 simple steps to protect your pet

1

2
