Frequently Asked Questions

Do finders need the PetNudge app? +
No! When someone taps the NFC tag or scans the QR code, they see a web page with your pet's info and a "Call Owner" button. No app needed for them.
What if I change my phone number? +
Just update your contact info in the PetNudge app. The tag links to your online profile, so changes appear instantly. No need to reprogram the tag!
Are NFC stickers waterproof? +
Most NFC stickers are water-resistant. For extra protection, place them inside a 3D printed holder or use epoxy-coated tags.
Can I use any NFC tag? +
We recommend NTAG215 or NTAG216 tags. They have enough memory for URLs and are widely compatible with all smartphones.
