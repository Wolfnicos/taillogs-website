The Problem

Why vaccination tracking matters

Keeping your pet's vaccinations up to date is one of the most important things you can do as a pet owner, but it's easy to fall behind.

📊

30% of pets are behind on vaccinations

Studies show that nearly one in three pets is not current on their core vaccinations. Busy schedules, lost paperwork, and confusing booster timelines all contribute to the problem. A single missed vaccine can leave your pet vulnerable to serious, sometimes fatal, diseases like parvovirus, distemper, or rabies.

📋

Paper records get lost or damaged

Vaccination cards from the vet can be misplaced, damaged by water, or forgotten in a drawer. When you need proof of vaccination for boarding, daycare, grooming, or international travel, scrambling to find records creates unnecessary stress. A digital vaccination tracker keeps everything accessible from your phone, anywhere you go.

🗓️

Booster schedules are confusing

Different vaccines have different schedules. Some require annual boosters, others every three years. Puppies and kittens need a series of shots at specific intervals. Without a system to track it all, it is easy to miss a critical window and have to restart the vaccination series from scratch.

Features

Everything you need to stay on top of vaccinations

PetNudge gives you a clear, organized view of every vaccination for every pet in your household.

🔴

Overdue Alerts

Vaccinations that are past their due date are flagged in red so you can see at a glance what needs immediate attention. Push notifications make sure you never miss the alert, even if you have not opened the app recently.

🟡

Due Soon Warnings

Approaching vaccinations are highlighted in yellow, giving you time to schedule an appointment before they become overdue. You choose the notification window: one week, two weeks, or a month ahead of the due date.

View all vaccinations sorted by urgency. Overdue items appear first, followed by due soon, and then up-to-date shots. This prioritized view makes it easy to decide which vet appointment to book next.

Store the name and details of the administering veterinarian alongside each vaccination record. When you switch vets or need to share your pet's history, every detail is already recorded and ready to share.

Batch Numbers

Record vaccine batch numbers and manufacturers for complete traceability. In the rare event of a vaccine recall or adverse reaction, you have the exact batch information at your fingertips without needing to contact your vet.

Export a professional PDF document with your pet's complete vaccination history. Perfect for boarding facilities, groomers, dog parks, pet sitters, and international travel documentation. Share via email, AirDrop, or print.

How It Works

Start tracking in 3 simple steps

Setting up vaccination tracking takes less than two minutes. Here's how to get started.

1

Download PetNudge and create your pet's profile

Install PetNudge from the App Store and add your pet. Enter their name, breed, date of birth, and a photo. The app supports dogs, cats, and other companion animals. You can add as many pets as you like with an active subscription.

2

Log existing vaccinations

Add your pet's vaccination history. For each vaccine, enter the name, date administered, next due date, vet name, and batch number. PetNudge automatically color-codes each record: green if current, yellow if due soon, and red if overdue. You can enter past vaccinations from your paper records to build a complete digital history.

3

Receive reminders and stay current

PetNudge sends push notifications when vaccinations are approaching their due date. When it is time for a booster, you will know well in advance so you can schedule an appointment. After the vet visit, log the new vaccination and the cycle continues automatically. No more guessing, no more missed shots.
